WELCOME TO LANDMARK CHRIST GOSPEL MISSION

On behalf of the General Overseer, Landmark Christ Gospel Mission (LMCGM), Pastor S. M. Adeoluwa, I welcome you in the name of our Lord Jesus Christ to the website of Landmark Christ Gospel Mission, Abule-Egba, Lagos. My prayer is that you would be divinely visited as you visit our site in Jesus name.

We would be highly honoured to have you worship with us if and when you are in Lagos. As we work to spread God's kingdom across Lagos, the Nigeria, and the whole world, I can tell you that the Lord has a position for you in our midst if you are persuaded that He would like you to join us permanently. The word of the bible (Matt. 9:37) about the fewness of labourers in spite of the vastness of the harvest is still as true now as it was then.

I invite you to join us at Strong Tower as we lift up the name of Jesus Christ. God bless you.

Shalom.

Pastor S. M. Adeoluwa

(General Overseer)

  3. Prayer will be the key.

OUR PASTOR

As the shepherd of LANDMARK CHRIST GOSPEL MISSION, LAGOS, it is my utmost joy and privilege to guide and serve our congregation. I am deeply committed to creating an environment where people can grow in their faith, find solace in times of need, and develop a strong relationship with God.

With a deep commitment to faith, I guide and serve towards building a strong community of believers, and I offer personal support to foster belonging and growth.

Pastor Samuel Adeoluwa
